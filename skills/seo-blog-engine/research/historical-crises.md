# Verified Historical Indian Brand Copy Crises (2010-2026)

## Case 1: Tanishq - Ekatvam Campaign (2020)
- **Brand**: Tanishq (Tata Group)
- **Campaign**: Ekatvam (Unity)
- **Controversy Date**: October 2020
- **Linguistic/Cultural Trigger**: Inter-faith baby shower narrative triggering massive social media polarization and allegations of promoting specific cultural biases.
- **ASCI Chapter**: Chapter II (Decency & Public Sentiment)
- **Backlash Score**: 92%
- **Result**: Ad withdrawn, company released official clarification statement.

## Case 2: Manyavar - Kanyamaan Campaign (2021)
- **Brand**: Manyavar
- **Campaign**: Kanyamaan (Reimagining Kanyadaan)
- **Controversy Date**: September 2021
- **Linguistic/Cultural Trigger**: Questioning traditional wedding rituals (Kanyadaan) in favor of mutual respect (Kanyamaan). Backlash from traditionalists.
- **ASCI Chapter**: Chapter II
- **Backlash Score**: 78%
- **Result**: Polarization, ad kept active but triggered heated cultural debates.

## Case 3: Zomato - Kachra Campaign (2023)
- **Brand**: Zomato
- **Campaign**: Kachra (Recycling initiative on World Environment Day)
- **Controversy Date**: June 2023
- **Linguistic/Cultural Trigger**: Depicting a character named "Kachra" (from the movie Lagaan, belonging to a marginalized community) literally as recycled items. Deemed highly classist and casteist.
- **ASCI Chapter**: Chapter II (Decency & Class Sensitivity)
- **Backlash Score**: 88%
- **Result**: Video deleted within hours, public apology issued.
