## Content Optimization Framework

### 1. Title Tag Formula
**Primary Keyword + Year + Brand Name + Value Proposition**

Examples:
- "Marketing Campaign Failures: Tanishq Ekatvam Crisis Case Study 2024 | BrandCheck Pro"
- "Brand Crisis Examples India: Zomato Kachra Campaign Backlash Analysis"
- "Advertising Controversies: FabIndia Jashn-e-Riwaaz Linguistic Risk Breakdown"

### 2. Meta Description Formula
**Hook (Problem) + Solution Preview + CTA + Character Count (150-160)**

Example:
> Discover why the Tanishq Ekatvam campaign sparked nationwide backlash. Learn key brand safety lessons from this inter-faith advertising controversy. Read the full analysis now.

### 3. Heading Structure
```
H1: Primary Keyword + Case Study Name
  H2: What Happened (The Campaign Context)
    H3: The Creative Concept
    H3: Target Audience & Market
  H2: Why It Failed (The Crisis Analysis)
    H3: Cultural Sensitivity Violations
    H3: Social Media Amplification
    H3: Regulatory Response (ASCI)
  H2: Impact & Consequences
    H3: Brand Reputation Damage
    H3: Financial Implications
    H3: Campaign Recall Process
  H2: Lessons for Brand Managers
    H2: How BrandCheck Pro Prevents Such Crises
  H2: Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ Schema)
```

## Image SEO Strategy

### Image Requirements Per Post
1. **Hero Image** (1200x630px) - Featured image with backlash score
2. **Campaign Screenshot** - Original controversial ad (fair use)
3. **Infographic** - Timeline of crisis escalation
4. **Analysis Chart** - Risk breakdown visualization
5. **Social Proof** - Tweet/media coverage collage

### Image Naming Convention
```
brandcheckpro-[brand]-[campaign]-[descriptor]-[year].webp

Examples:
- brandcheckpro-tanishq-ekatvam-campaign-ad-2020.webp
- brandcheckpro-zomato-kachra-controversy-tweet-2023.webp
- brandcheckpro-fabindia-jashn-e-riwaaz-backlash-timeline-2021.webp
```

### Alt Text Formula
**[Event] + [Visual Description] + [BrandCheck Pro Analysis]**

Examples:

- "FabIndia Jashn-e-Riwaaz Diwali collection promotional tweet"

## GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) Strategy

### For AI Chatbots (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity)
1. **Clear Entity Relationships**: Explicitly connect brands to controversies
2. **Structured Data**: Tables comparing campaign metrics
3. **Definition Lists**: Key terms explained
4. **Bullet Point Summaries**: Easy extraction for AI
5. **Direct Answers**: Clear, quotable statements

### Target AI Queries
- "What was the Tanishq Ekatvam controversy?"
- "Why was Zomato Kachra campaign banned?"
- "Examples of failed marketing campaigns in India"
- "Brand crisis case studies with analysis"
- "ASCI advertising violations examples"

### Citation Strategy
- Link to authoritative sources (ASCI, news outlets)
- Include publication dates
- Reference official statements
- Cite academic research (IIMC thesis)
